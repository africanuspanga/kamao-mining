# CLIENT CONTENT REQUIRED

This document lists every item that KAMAO Mining Company Limited must provide, verify or approve before it can be published on the website. The website has been built so that all of these fields are optional or hidden until verified information is supplied.

**Do not invent or publish any of the items below without written client approval and supporting documentation.**

---

## Corporate identity and legal information

- [ ] Founding year / date established
- [ ] Legal registration number and jurisdiction
- [ ] Full headquarters / registered office address
- [ ] Mine ownership structure
- [ ] Number of employees and contractors
- [ ] Approved company history timeline with verifiable dates

## Mining licences and concessions

- [ ] Mining licence numbers and issuing authority
- [ ] Concession / permit areas and coordinates
- [ ] Licence status, grant dates and renewal dates
- [ ] Any legal or regulatory approvals relevant to publication

## Projects and operations

- [ ] Verified project names
- [ ] Province and exact location of each project
- [ ] Minerals present at each project
- [ ] Development stage of each project
- [ ] Project descriptions approved for public use
- [ ] Genuine project photographs with usage rights
- [ ] Production volumes, throughput or capacity figures
- [ ] Mineral grades, reserve estimates or resource estimates
- [ ] Workforce numbers per project
- [ ] Project ownership percentages

## Commercial and partnerships

- [ ] Customer or offtake partner names (only if approved for public disclosure)
- [ ] Export destinations or primary markets
- [ ] Verified partnership names and descriptions
- [ ] Supplier or technical-service partner names (if approved)
- [ ] Revenue, financial or investment information

## Certifications, compliance and approvals

- [ ] Certifications (e.g., ISO, responsible-mining standards)
- [ ] Government approvals relevant to marketing content
- [ ] Kimberley Process compliance documentation (before any diamond-related claims)
- [ ] OECD due-diligence or traceability programme documentation
- [ ] Any claims related to "conflict-free", "fully traceable" or equivalent

## Responsibility and sustainability

- [ ] Verified health-and-safety records and statistics
- [ ] Environmental management policies and monitoring results
- [ ] Verified sustainability achievements
- [ ] Community-investment figures and programmes
- [ ] Local procurement or employment statistics
- [ ] Ethics and whistleblowing channel details

## Leadership and team

- [ ] Full leadership names and approved titles
- [ ] Professional biographies
- [ ] Approved portrait photographs with usage rights
- [ ] LinkedIn profiles (where applicable)

## Media and brand assets

- [ ] Final approved logo in horizontal, stacked and icon-only formats
- [ ] Reversed / white version of the logo
- [ ] Favicon package
- [ ] Genuine photographs of company operations, teams and sites
- [ ] Hero background video (if desired) with usage rights and a static fallback image
- [ ] Approved social-media profile URLs (before icons are displayed)

## Contact and recruitment

- [ ] Verified physical address for display and map
- [ ] Dedicated recruitment email or application system
- [ ] Preferred third-party contact-form endpoint (optional)
- [ ] Dedicated ethics / whistleblowing email (optional)

## General

- [ ] Any awards, testimonials or case studies with approval and attribution
- [ ] Any other information currently shown as `[CLIENT TO PROVIDE VERIFIED INFORMATION]` in the codebase

---

## How to use this list

1. Provide only information that has been verified internally and approved for public communication.
2. Share supporting documents or source files where applicable.
3. The development team will populate the relevant content files and enable hidden sections once the content is received.
4. Until content is supplied, the website will either hide the relevant component or display an internal placeholder that does not appear on the public site.
